### Question 1: 
Consider the following figure of a ball pictured at various times while rolling some ramps. The time of each instance is shown above the ball. Assume the \textit{middle segment of the track} is made from a 2 meters long wooden cutout and that the ball started from rest at 0.3 seconds. Note: The drawing it \textit{not} to scale and you do not know the length of any of the segments other than the middle one!

a) Find the velocity of the ball at 2.3 seconds.  
b) Find the acceleration of the ball while it was traveling down the first ramp.  
c) Using your results from parts (a) and (b), find the velocity of the ball at 3.1 seconds.
