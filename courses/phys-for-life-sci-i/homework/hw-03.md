### Question 1: 
The positions of objects A and B are graphed to the right. Answer the following questions and explain. 
	
a) Is the acceleration of object A positive, negative, or zero at $t = 2\,\text{s}$?

c) Is the acceleration of object B positive, negative, or zero at $t = 2\,\text{s}$? 

d) Do the two objects ever have the same position? If so, at what time?

e) Which object has the greater speed at $t=11\,\text{s}$

f) Find the average velocity of object B for the 20-second interval shown.
