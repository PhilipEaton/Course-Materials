<strong>Volume</strong>: An object fully submerged in a fluid will displace a volume of fluid equal to its own volume.<br>


<strong>Density</strong>: $ \rho = \fac{m}{V}$  <br>
<strong>Pressure</strong>: $ P = \fac{F}{A}$ <br>
<br>

$m$: mass <br>
$V$: volume <br>
$F$: force <br>
$A$: area 


</div>

<div class="column">


<strong>Pressure at Depth:</strong>
$$
P_2 = P_1 + \rho_\text{fluid} \, g \, h
$$

$P_2$: pressure at depth <br>
$P_1$: pressure at surface <br>
$\rho_\text{fluid}$: density of fluid <br>
$g$: gravitational strength <br>
$h$: depth 


</div>
