### Example 07
A toy drag car has front and rear tires of radii 0.1 m and 0.2 m, respectively. A child pushes the car such that is goes from rest to moving at 5 m/s by the time the front tires have rotated though and angle of 25 radians.

a) What are the angular velocities of the front and back tires when the car is moving at 5 m/s?  
b) What was the average angular acceleration of the front tires while the car was speeding up?  
c) What was the average translational acceleration of the car?  
d) Through what angle did the rear tires turn while the car was speeding up?  
e) How far did the car travel by the time it reaches the speed of 5 m/s?  
f) How many second did it take the car to reach the speed of 5 m/s?
