### Example 03
The Bobsled jump was a new event at the 2002 Winter Olympic Games in Park City, Utah (not really). The bobsled (+ occupants) are launched (at point A) by a large spring onto a huge semi-circular track ( 100 m) of frictionless ice. The bobsled teams try to achieve the furthest landing distance on a level landing field below.

a) Find the work done by the ramp on the bobsled as it moves from point A to point B.  
b) The mass of the bobsled+team is 800 kg. If they are traveling at a speed of 50 m/s when they reach point B, find the spring constant for the spring. Assume the spring was initially compressed by 4 m.  
c) How fast will the bobsled+team (800 kg) be going right before they hit the ground to the right?
