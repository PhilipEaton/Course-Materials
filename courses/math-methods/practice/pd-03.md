## Classifications
For each of the following differential equations,

$$
\begin{aligned}
&y'' + 3y' - 4y = 0\\
&y' = y \cos(t)\\
&x^2 y'' + xy' + y = \sin(x)\\
&y' = y^2 + 1
\end{aligned}
$$

identify them along the following citeria:

- The order of the equation.
- Whether it is linear or nonlinear.
- Whether it is homogeneous or inhomogeneous.
- Whether the coefficients are constant or variable.

## Exact Equation Check
Consider the equation:

$$
(2xy + \cos y)\,dx + (x^2 - y\sin y)\,dy = 0
$$

Determine whether this equation is exact. If it is, solve it. If not, explain why and suggest what kind of integrating factor (in terms of $x$ or $y$) might help.
