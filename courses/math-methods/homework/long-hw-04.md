### 1) Identifying ODE Types
For each of the following equations, 

$$ 
y'' + 3xy' - 2y = e^x \qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad x^2 \frac{d^2y}{dx^2} + x \frac{dy}{dx} - y = \sin x 
$$

$$ 
y' + y^2 = x \qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial y^2} = 0 
$$

classify them by:
- Order
- Whether they are linear or nonlinear
- Whether they are homogeneous or inhomogeneous
- Whether they have constant or variable coefficients
- Whether they are ordinary or partial differential equations




### 2) Separation of Variables
Solve the following ODEs using separation of variables.

$$ 
\text{a)} \qquad  \frac{dy}{dx} = x e^{-y} \qquad\qquad \text{b)} \qquad  \frac{dx}{dt} = \frac{t}{x} \qquad\qquad \text{c)} \qquad  \frac{dN}{dt} = kN(1 - N) 
$$

> Hint for part c) Consider using partial fractions to make the integral easier to complete. 


### 3) Integrating Factors
Solve the following first-order linear ODEs using the integrating factor method.

$$ 
\text{a)} \qquad  \frac{dy}{dx} + y = e^{-x} \qquad\qquad \text{b)} \qquad  x \frac{dy}{dx} + 2y = x^2 \quad\text{for}\quad x > 0 \qquad\qquad \text{c)} \qquad  \frac{dy}{dx} + \frac{y}{x} = \ln(x) 
$$

 $$

### 5) Reducible Second-Order ODE
Make a variable substitution to reduce this second-order ODE to a first-order ODE:

$$
y''(x) = y'(x)
$$

Solve the resulting first order ODE and then get the solution for $y(x)$

### 6) Variation of Parameters for a First-Order ODE

$$
\frac{dy}{dx} + 2y = e^{-x}
$$



### 7) Steady State

a) Population growth in a confined environment.

$$ \frac{dP}{dt} = kP(1 - P/K) $$

b) Electrical circuit with resistance $ R $ and voltage $ V $.

$$ \frac{dQ}{dt} + RQ = V $$
